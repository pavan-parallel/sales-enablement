Part two

Meet Sequoia.

Our design system. We named it after the tree, because it's built to last.

Sequoia

The system is built in layers.

Small pieces combine into bigger ones, all the way up to full pages.

FoundationsColour, type, spacing.
→
AtomsButtons, fields, toggles.
→
MoleculesLabelled inputs, rows, menus.
→
ComponentsTables, drawers, wizards.
→
TemplatesFull pages, ready to fill.

Sequoia

Change one thing, and it updates everywhere.

Colours, type and spacing are saved as reusable settings. Change one, and it flows through the whole product at once.
