Think roofing, pest control, solar. Everyone from sales reps to hiring managers to admins works out of the same product.

Visual language

None of it was random.

We chose indigo.
It feels calm and in control, which matters when the screen is about someone's pay.
We chose a typeface that works everywhere.
Clear in a table full of numbers, still friendly on a welcome screen.
We gave everything room to breathe.
