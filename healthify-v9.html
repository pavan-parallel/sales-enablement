What we shipped Healthify × Parallel

Tracking became the primary action

People come to Healthify to track their food, so it's the first thing they see now.

Logging sits at the top of the screen, impossible to miss.
Frequently tracked and saved meals make logging even faster.

Snap, search or speak are no longer separate tools

They open from one place and hand off to each other so logging never breaks step.

Switch between modalities without losing context

You can start the log by speaking and finish with typing.

Every score comes with a why

Ria grades the meal with a single number and explains the reasoning behind it.

Insights that tell you what to do next

How the week went, in words, so you don't have to interpret graphs.
