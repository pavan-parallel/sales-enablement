02 · The language

Nothing in it is decoration.

Indigo, for calm authority over money. Hanken Grotesk, legible in a table of numbers and friendly in a welcome screen. Generous spacing, because admins live here all day. Every choice traces back to the brief.

AtomsButtons, fields, toggles, tags, icons.

→
ComponentsTables, drawers, wizards, date pickers, workspaces.
→
TemplatesFull page patterns, ready to fill.

03 · Sequoia

Tokens, not hex codes.

Every decision lives as a named variable. Change it once, it updates everywhere.

Font Family/Primary → Hanken Grotesk
Color/Buttons/primary_default → #4E61D6
Color/Text/text_error → #8E0B16
Spacing/spacing_16 → 16  ·  Radius/full → 9999
Paragraph/Small/Medium → Primary · 14 · 500 · 1.5

Pulled from the real file. The names are the documentation.

03 · Sequoia

Responsive by default.

Auto layout everywhere. Components resize, reflow and hold their alignment as content changes, so a screen built today survives the feature added next year.

04 · The stress test

We rebuilt onboarding, the hardest flow in the product, in Sequoia.

Commission-based pay, different for every product a rep sells. One product's pay used to live in three separate steps. Sequoia's patterns regrouped it: one page per product, eight steps down to six.

The rebuilt Product payment page
Placeholder · screenshot

04 · The stress test

Where a real screen broke a component, we fixed the system, not the screen.

Payroll tables, commission wizards, document signing: each one pushed on Sequoia, and each fix landed in the system. Every flow shipped made it sturdier.

06 · The handoff

Built to be extended without us in the room.

Semantic naming, usage guidance, and a structure that tells you where new things go. The system assumes we won't be there, and is written so nobody has to ask.

06 · The handoff

Then Sequifi extended the engagement to the entire platform.

After the system and the first flows, they asked us to take Sequoia across every module. The strongest review a design system can get.

A design system is a bet on everything you'll build after it. Sequoia is how we place that bet.
