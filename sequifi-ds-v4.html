Sequifi is HR and payroll software for home-services companies.

Think roofing, pest control, solar. Everyone from sales reps to hiring managers to admins works out of the same product.

Visual language

Every choice inside Opscape had a reason.

Calm under pressure, so we chose indigo.
One confident colour for the action that matters, quiet everywhere else.
One typeface that works in two very different places.
Warm on a welcome, exact in a table full of numbers. That's Hanken Grotesk.
Made for all-day use, so we gave everything room.
Generous spacing for people who never leave these screens.
