Chapter 03 · The explorationHealthify × Parallel

The exploration

We explored twelve divergent concepts to solve the problems we'd discovered.

Each concept went through testing with a fresh mix of users.

Testing helped us set the priorities: what to build, what to explore later, what to hold.

Tested with a fresh participant mix: existing all-active users, next-gen users, and new users, from metros to small towns. The goal was to separate genuine signal from novelty.

Personalised meal planning

The clear winner — 'Picked for you' was the most popular concept in the study, 11 of 13 keen.

Multi-modal & assistive

Users are loyal to one modality, chosen for accuracy. Every new modality must earn its trust.

Community & social

Peers' preferences, celebrity diets and polls failed to resonate. Curiosity, not utility.

The roadmap was rebuilt around the five strongest concepts.
