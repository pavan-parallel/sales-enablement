Sequifi makes the software home-services companies use to hire, pay, and manage their workers. Roofing, pest control, solar.
Its users are field sales reps and the people who manage them. Usually young, often on a first job, rarely sitting at a desk.
They live in social apps and drop anything that feels like old software. Sequifi had to feel as easy as the apps they already use.
The product had found its market. But a lot of HR still ran off the platform, in spreadsheets. To grow, Sequifi came to Parallel for a design system, and a simpler way to onboard workers.
Home servicesRoofing, pest control, solar, lawn care

Chapter 04

The turn

The plan was to hand it off.

The turnThe plan was for Sequifi to take the system and redesign the product themselves.
The turnIt didn't go that way. Years of features had piled up, and applying a clean system to a complicated product was harder than it looked.
The turnSo they came back with a smaller ask. Could we redesign one module, as an example?
The turnThat one module became the entire platform.
