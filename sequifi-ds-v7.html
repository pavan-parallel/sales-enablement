Sequifi is HR and payroll software for home-services companies.

And it runs the whole back office operation across many industries.

·One back office

Two very different kinds of people depend on it.

The HR admin the key user

1Runs hiring, payroll and payments for the whole operation.

2Doesn't come from a tech background, and rarely has a spare hour to learn a new tool.

So the whole operation rests on one person who has no room to spare.

The rep out in the field

1Young and barely tech-literate. Primarily lives on social media apps.

2In the job for the money.

They churn fast, and if the tool is hard, they simply never learn it.

A single admin runs pay for hundreds of reps, every week.

They stare at the tool five or six hours a day. One small mistake means reps get paid wrong, and reps who get paid wrong leave.

five or six hours a day

Over the years, features piled up.

Screens got dense, and no two looked alike.

The components were inconsistent.

The same button or form was rebuilt in every module, so every screen meant learning it again.

Twelve “Save” buttons, from twelve modules. Illustrative recreation of the mismatch.

There was no visual hierarchy.

Everything looked equally important, so it was easy to miss what mattered.

So Sequifi has to be strict about the money and warm about the people, at the same time.

We turned that into one visual language.

Calm, confident and uncluttered. Precision for the admin, simplicity for the rep.

Indigo keeps the screen calm and the next action obvious.

It is the one colour we use for all the action you should take. Everything else stays black, white and grey, so nothing competes with what you came to do.

A system is only as good as what happens next.

Sequifi × Parallel · How we work · 04 / 04

Working with us feels light.

Open files

You see it as it's built. Our working files stay open the whole time, so there are no surprises at handoff.

Senior-led

The people who pitch do the work. Senior designers on the project from day one, not a hand-off to juniors.

Async

Nothing waits for a meeting. Walkthroughs you can watch whenever you want, so the work keeps moving.

Your developers don't wait for us to finish.We share the foundations first, so the build starts while the rest of the system comes together.
